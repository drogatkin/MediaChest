Subject: What is recommended way to enter my media files to be organized by MediaChest?

You can fall in one of group of potential users of MediaChest:

  1. You just bought a digital camera and want to move taken pictures in a computer see section (2-1)
  2. You already own a camera for awhile and want to organize already taken pictures
  3. You have no digital camera, but you have image files and want to organize them

Subject: How to enter picture from a digital camera?

The current version does not support direct communication to a digital camera, although this possibility is my plans. When the program started developed, only serial protocol was supported and it was extremely slow and not reliable, so the program was oriented to use flush card readers. Most of flush card readers will create a virtual disk presenting your card. You need to specify this disk
